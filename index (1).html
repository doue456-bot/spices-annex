Our Story

More Than a
Restaurant

Spices Annex was founded on a simple belief: great food and great company go hand in hand. We source fresh ingredients daily, crafting dishes that celebrate bold, authentic flavours from local and regional traditions.

Beyond dining, we offer thoughtfully designed spaces for meetings, workshops, and private events — because we know that some of the best ideas are born over a good meal.
